In the recent times


Opening up

Crimes against women in India is seen to be steadily rising. More than 32,500 cases of rape were registered with the police in 2017, about 90 a day, according to the most recent government data.

Indian courts disposed of only about 18,300 cases related to rape that year, leaving more than 127,800 cases pending at the end of 2017.

It is high time a reliable system is introduced that can help women travel independently and safely.


Women, like men, should try to do the impossible. And when they fail, their failure should be a challenge to others.

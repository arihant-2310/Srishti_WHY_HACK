About Srishti


Looking out

We are an organization focused on helping women navigate the world fearlessly. One of the biggest challenges faced by women worldwide is the unavailability of reliable sources to provide them with information regarding the safety of a particular location. Through our projects we hope to equip all women with the required, reliable tools so that there is nothing stopping them from achieving their dreams.

Srishti is a project that contains different modules which are joined together to help women conquer the world. It consists of a map that shows the safety of a particular location on a map, a chatbot to help the users clear any queries they may have, a discussion forum to prove the trustability of the service and many more features .
